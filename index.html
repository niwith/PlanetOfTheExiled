Welcome

Planet of The Exiled is an RTS like game with a twist. Rather than playing individual missions the entire game occurs on one map. The idea of the game as it stands at this time is you will control a small community of people living on a planet that has drifted away from its star. This group of people live around one of the few locations of heat activity on the surface of the planet, however when it runs out of energy and begins to cool the people must journey to another location where they can settle. Your goal is to keep them alive in the harsh wasteland and lead them to their new home. You will have to manage their food, defenses, fuel, heat and building components (subject to change). You will encounter many obstacles to overcome such as wasteland bandits, animals, lack of food, cold etc.
